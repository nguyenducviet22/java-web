Story 1: Making noodles


Zhou Haisheng is eight years old this year, and his father's name is Lao Zhou. ao Zhou's dishes are so delicious that he opened a restaurant in Shanghai called "Zhou's Restaurant". The hotel is not big, but there are many people every day. The restaurant has rice, noodles, and many dishes.

Lao Zhou's hometown is in Shanxi. Lao Zhou and his wife came to Shanghai to open this restaurant. His son was born in Shanghai and his name is Zhou Haisheng.

When Haisheng was a child, he went to the restaurant with his parents every day. Their family eats at the restaurant every day and closes the door late every day to go home. It can be said that the restaurant is Haisheng's second home.
